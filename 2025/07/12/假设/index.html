Assumptions

There are three stages of development:
First, people don’t trust you.
Second, they don’t obey you.
Third, they are no longer afraid of you.

假设

发展分为三个阶段：
第一阶段，人们不信任你；
第二阶段，他们不服从你；
第三阶段，他们已经不再害怕你。
