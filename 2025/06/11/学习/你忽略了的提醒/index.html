你忽略了的提醒

太晚了

今天我突然明白了，一个人最大的财富，就是出生在一个正常的地方，或者一个正常的家庭。

教育

教育是民族最伟大的生存原则，

教育是一切社会把恶的数量减少，

教育是把善的数量增多的唯一的手段。

——奥诺雷·巴尔扎克

回顾

一个有创造力的人不会沉溺于过去的痛苦，他们会学会教训；而弱者则是整日沉浸在痛苦里，回顾以往的苦难来折磨自己。

——奥诺雷·巴尔扎克 ​​​

除非是为了从过去的错误中吸取有益的教训，并从来之不易的经验中获益，否则我们不应该回顾过去。–华盛顿

真相

当真相被埋葬时，它仍在生长，虽然被压抑，却积聚着这样一种力量，一旦爆发，它将炸毁一切，灰飞烟灭。

——爱弥尔·左拉

Reminders you ignored

Too late

Today, I suddenly understood that the greatest fortune in life is being born in a normal place or into a normal family.

Education

Education is the greatest living principle of a nation, and it can also reduce the number of crimes in society. It is the only way to increase kindness. — Balzac

Review

A creative person cannot be consumed by past pain. They learn from their failures. But a weak person wallows in pain every day and allows it to torment them. — Balzac

We shouldn’t dwell on the past—unless we seek to learn from our failures and gain valuable lessons from them. — Washington

truth

The truth would still grow up after it got buried. The truth was building up a kind of power when it got depressed . Once it broke out ## it would wipe out everything and reduce to ashes. –Said by Emile Zola
