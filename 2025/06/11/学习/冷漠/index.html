冷漠

上船不思岸上人，下船不提船上事。

旧人无需知近况，新人不必问过往。

这是谁说的？

谁能做到如此的绝情？

Indifference

One who has journeyed upon the sea seldom reflects on those left ashore. One who remains on land rarely speaks of the trials at sea.

A newcomer should refrain from inquiring about bygone days.

Who first uttered these words?

And who, indeed, can accomplish the truly unforeseen?
